The Language Detection algorithm is based on the letter frequency.

The sample text is compared to all the languages based on the letter frequency with the distance function:

sum for_each_language_letter ( language_letter_freq - sample_text_letter_freq) ^ 2

The information on letter frequency for each language is taken from internet from the following web-sites:
